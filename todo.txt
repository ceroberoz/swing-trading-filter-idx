# Future Development Plan - Swing Trading Filter (IDX)

## ‚úÖ COMPLETED FEATURES

### Strategy & Analysis
- **Backtesting Engine** - Test strategies on past data to see what works
  - View win rate, profit potential, and risk metrics
  - Generate performance charts and detailed reports
  - Command: --backtest, --start-date, --end-date, --detailed, --charts

- **Multi-Timeframe Analysis** - Check both daily and weekly trends
  - Weekly trend alignment (10/30 moving averages)
  - Shows "W.Trend" column (UP/DOWN)

- **Market Condition Filter** - Identifies overall market health
  - Detects bull market (RISK_ON) vs bear market (RISK_OFF) conditions
  - Based on Jakarta Composite Index (^JKSE) trend
  - Affects signal quality scoring

- **Support & Resistance Levels** - Key price zones for entry/exit planning
  - Pivot points (price levels where trends may reverse)
  - 20-day high/low swing levels
  - Shown in "S/R" column

- **Investment Recommendations** - Clear action suggestions
  - BUY ALL, BUY PARTIAL, HOLD, SELL PARTIAL, SELL ALL
  - Based on signal strength, trend, market conditions, and price position

### Filtering & Quality Control
- **Market Cap Filter** - Skip very small stocks (minimum 10 trillion IDR)
  - Reduces exposure to highly volatile penny stocks
  - Configurable threshold in config.py

- **Volume Filter** - Requires 1.2x average volume
  - Ensures sufficient trading activity
  - Improves signal reliability

- **Optimized Parameters** - Fine-tuned for Indonesian market
  - Moving averages: 13/34 periods
  - RSI thresholds: 75 (overbought) / 35 (oversold)
  - Stop-loss: 2.0x ATR (Average True Range - volatility measure)
  - Target: 3-12% profit

### Watchlist Management
- **Multiple Watchlists** - Pre-configured stock lists
  - default.txt - Your personal picks
  - lq45.txt - Blue chip stocks (LQ45 index)
  - idx_liquid.txt - 100+ actively traded stocks
  - Command: --list [name]
